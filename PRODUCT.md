# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Parents of prospective SMP/MTs students, the students themselves, and local community members seeking school information.

## Product Purpose

Present SMKIT Ushuluddin's institutional identity, communicate its dual‑curriculum strength, and drive SPMB 2026/2027 registrations.

## Brand Personality

Tenang, Profesional, Berwibawa.

## Anti-references

Sales‑y urgency pages, stock‑photo templates, over‑animated hero sections, excessive CTA clutter.

## Design Principles

1. Photography‑first
2. Whitespace = confidence
3. Editorial typography hierarchy
4. One CTA per section
5. Teal accent used sparingly

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG 2.1 AA minimum; respect prefers‑reduced‑motion.
